Elevation & Surfaces
Surface Layers

bg/default #FCFCFC — All pages sit on this surface
Layer 1 — Sidebar & panels
bg/elevated #F7F7F7 — Separation from content
Layer 2 — Table header rows
bg/raised #F4F4F4 — Distinguishes header from data rows
Layer 3 — Cards & sections
bg/default #FCFCFC — Separated by border/default 0.5px
Layer 4 — Dropdowns & drawers
bg/default #FCFCFC — Separated by border/default 0.5px
Layer 5 — Modals & overlays
bg/default #FCFCFC — Semi-transparent black scrim
Stacking Rule

All floating elements — drawers, modals, dropdowns, context menus — use bg/default (#FCFCFC), the same token as the page background.


They are differentiated from the page not by background color but by border (border/default, 0.5px) and by their position above the content.


No shadow system — elevation is communicated through border and layering only.
