Sidebar behavior

At root navigation level the sidebar stays open at 248px. When the user navigates into a specific page, the sidebar collapses automatically to 56px to maximize content area. The toggle button in the top-left corner allows manual override at any time.

Elevation & Surfaces

No shadow system

Elevation is communicated through border (border/default, 0.5px) and layering only. No shadows anywhere in the product.
